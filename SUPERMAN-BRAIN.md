# The Superman Brain — founding prompt (the north star)

*Read this first, always. Paste it at the top of any build session (Cursor, Fable,
Manus) so the work never drifts from what this is for. This is why; the rest of
`docs/` is how.*

---

You are building **Lindsey Conrad's Superman Brain** — one private operating system
that runs her whole world so she can grow Goldfront + Conrad Enterprises, make
millions, and actually enjoy her life instead of worrying about money or work.

**What it is:** not a dashboard, not a chatbot — a **standing board of the most
experienced executives**, working together for Lindsey around the clock. It finds
everything, sees everything she's doing, makes the calls she wouldn't think to make,
finds money she'd miss, and hands her the few moves that matter — so she can look at
one screen and feel **"we got this."**

**What it must do:**
- **Find money she'd miss** — untapped flywheel touches, deals that hit her buy-box,
  margin leaks, follow-ups nobody sent. Surface the move even when her own habit
  would skip it.
- **Watch for holes and people not doing the right thing** — cross-reference what
  people said they'd do (Fieldy, ClickUp, recorded calls, email) against what
  actually moved, and flag who's behind before it costs her.
- **Reduce her overwhelm** — one calm screen, the top 3 things that matter today.
- **Run from anywhere** — mobile-first, fast on Starlink from the Cybertruck.
- **Give her a daily brief from Fieldy** — she wears it all day; reconstruct her day each morning.
- **Cover the whole person** — business seats AND personal: training, mind/wellbeing,
  personal CFO, health (Apple Health, Whoop, supplements, peptides, diet), calendar
  protection, relationships.

**How it thinks:** Hormozi (offers), Donald Miller/StoryBrand (messaging), Simon
Sinek (why / long game), Dan Martell (buy back her time) — in her voice, never quoting.

**End-state:** whole team on ClickUp, everyone on GHL with calls recorded; Lindsey
sees everything daily and issues tasks while she travels.

**Hard rules:** engine computes numbers (Brain narrates, never invents) · nothing
sends without her approval · escalate, don't guess · credit-adjacent is internal-facing
only · health seats track/remind/route to real pros, never prescribe · recent decisions
outweigh old.

**Non-negotiables:** build once (durable) · her brain first and private (Conrad
Command Center = Lindsey's; Goldfront = base for the other owners; data never mixes) ·
connect at her pace — works with whatever's connected, clean "connect this" for the
rest, never forces connecting everything at once.

**Build order:** 1 Brain core → 2 Training loop → 3 Shadow validation (~20 real calls)
→ 4 Command Center → 5 deploy + live connectors. Earn the room before you build the room.

When a detail is unclear, choose the option that makes Lindsey more successful, more in
control, and less overwhelmed.
